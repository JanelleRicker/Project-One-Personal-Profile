I'm Janelle.

Running and designing in Indy.

I run.

Once in an interview I responded to a question about how I handle stress with two words - "I run." As I look back, I can only hope I clarified that statement by explaining that I was refering to physical exercise. Years have passed since I gave that two word answer. I traded running in a small Ohio town for running the hills of Pennslvania. Now I run my west Indianapolis neighbor. I've had a fracture in my ankle. My running partner had to quit because of reconstructive surgery to his foot.

Things have changed. But I still want those two words to be true - I run.

I design.

I enjoy creating in variety of media. Whether I have my foot on the pedal of my sewing machine, a paint brush in my hand, or my eyes on a computer screen, I love to design. Check out the Designs page to see some of my digital work.

The digital world continues to grow and define how we do business and see the world. I would like add my voice to the digital design chorus. That's why I'm learning to create websites.

One day I hope to use the two words above to describe my occupation. I can hear it now. "So Janelle, what do you do?" "I design."

I enjoy.

Other things I enjoy include serving with my church family, camping, board games, reading historic non-fiction, and movies.
